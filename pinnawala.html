PINNAWALA

PINNAWALA ELEPHANT ORPHANAGE

For all you elephant lover, Pinnawala elephant orphanage is the best place to go to a unique never before face on experience with elephants like no other. This unique sanctuary is home to many elephants where they are taken care of. This orphanage does not just have local reach but its well known all over the world.

This great sanctuary is a huge remote area filled with many trees,shruds,herbs so the elephants can feast all they want.93 elephants currently inhabit these grounds where they are taken care of. Maya oya being located close by brings an additional value to this beautiful habitat.

Located in Rambukkana,in Kegalle city, established by the Sri Lanka Wildlife department, covers almost 24 acres. Also know as the best herd of elephants in captivity all around the world is also a breeding place for elephants where they live peacefully. Tourists can feed the baby elephants, join the morning and afternoon baths and enjoy a good full day among them.

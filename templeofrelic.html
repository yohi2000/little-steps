THE SACRED TOOTH RELIC

THE TEMPLE

Temple of Sacred Tooth Relic is a renowned place of worship by the Buddhist community. The tooth of Lord Buddha is displayed there for devotees to worship. This was names as a world heritage by the UNESCO in 1988 as devotees from all over the world visits this place for blessings. This is one of the most significant and cultural destination in Sri Lanka. The unique Kandyan architecture style was used when building this destination. This is known as the “ Dalada Maligawa”. This name was given to this because the architecture used was the same as a kandyan “ Dalada Mandiraya”

TWO GREAT MAGISTRIES

The tooth relic is closely associated with the Malwana and Asgiriya monastic establishments. The thre custorials of the Tooth Relic are Diyawadana NIlame, and the 2 manayakas. The 2 Mahanayakas are considered as the guardians of Sri Lanka and are currently provides blessings in the practice of the state policies. The 2 monkish avocations, gamavasi and vanavasi are being followed by the two monistic establishments

CHIEF CUSTODIAN

The chief custodian of the temple in the main bearer of all proceedings that happen. Organizing and carrying out rituals, as well as maintain all property of the temple is managed by him in the most honorable way. Welcoming tourists and other foreign officials is also a duty he bears. Dr.Padeep Nilanga Dela is the current custodian of the temple. He has immense experience as he was a former Nilame prior.

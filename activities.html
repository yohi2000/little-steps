COLOMBO NATIONAL MUSEUM



Colombo national museum is a compilation of antiques and cultural monuments that can be used to see the eventful history of Sri Lanka. It was built in 1877 and is a portrayal of the rich heritage of Sri Lanka. There are many paintings, royal objects, art crafts, statues etc.

YALA SAFARI



It is the most famous national park in Sri Lanka. It is situated in the south East and has a great diversity of animals and birds. This is one of the main attractive points to spot a lepard who loves to sit on the huge boulders. It is divided into 5 blocks and has an area of nealy 130,00 hectares.

DUTCH FORT



It is located on the southern coast of Sri Lanka. It was built during the time when the Portuguese reigned in Sri Lanka. It is one of the most treasured historical monuments in Sri Lanka. It is a compilation of age-old architecture and has many restaurants, cafes, and exhibition halls.


FEES AND WHAT TO KNOW


  • Location: The Galle Dutch Fort is located at Church St, Galle 80000, Sri Lanka.
  • Entry fee: There is no entry fee for visiting the Galle Dutch Fort.
  • Timings: The Galle Dutch Fort is open 24 hours on all days.

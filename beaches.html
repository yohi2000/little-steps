BEACHES

HIKKADUWA BEACH


Since 1960, Hikkaduwa has been known as one of the most attractive beaches in Sri Lanka. The beach spread to a few kilometers. There are many activities that have been loved by tourists like snorkeling and scuba diving. The clear water attracts many people who love the sea and its vibrant aquatic life. One of the main attracting point of hikkaduwa is the corals that are enriched with many sea creatures. Hikkaduwa is also enriched with many seafood cuisines that are enriched with flavor.

TRINCOMALEE BEACH


This is a beach that is stretched across the northern stretch of trinco and is nearly 30 km. The main tourist attractions in Trinco are Nilavali beach, pigeon island, natural habour, and the snorkeling hotspot of the east. There are unspoiled shallow waters that are great for swimming. Trinco habour is one of the most finest natural harbours in the world. Another main attraction in trinco is the famous hindu temple called “koneswaran” situated at the top of a stunning cliff overlooking the great Indian ocean.

UNAWATUNA BEACH


This is the biggest tourist destination in Sri Lanka. It is a banana-shaped beach that is known as the most famous beach in Sri Lanka and one of the main tourist destinations. The best time to visit the beach is either from December to April or July to September. It is known for its golden sand is turquoise water.
